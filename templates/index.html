What is Stock?

A stock represents ownership in a company and constitutes a claim on part of the company’s assets and earnings. Investors buy stocks to potentially gain profits from the company’s growth and dividends. The stock market allows individuals and institutions to trade shares of publicly-listed companies. Understanding stocks is essential for making informed investment decisions and participating in the growth of businesses.
